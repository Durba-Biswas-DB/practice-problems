# Finding Good Days 

## Problem Description

Alex has a cat named **Boomer**. He decides to put his cat to the test for eternity.

- On **day 1**, there is **1 unit** of food stash.
- On **each subsequent day**, the stash **doubles**.
- Boomer receives food **only on days she is well behaved**.
- If she is well behaved on a day, she receives food equal to the stash of that day.

At the end, Boomer has received a **total of `A` units of food**.
